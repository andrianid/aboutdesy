A memory

There are many memories; some happy and some sad. But I’ll share one of the happiest memory I have.


I lived in Taiwan for 1 year. Besides going to college, I also participated in several off-campus activities. One of the most memorable and enjoyable experiences was when I volunteered at the National Batik Day commemoration there.


The event was held by the Indonesian Diaspora Network in Taiwan (IDN Taiwan). The event includes a batik workshop, a batik exhibition, and a fashion show. Everyone in Taiwan may attend this event, from Indonesian Diaspora, Taiwanese, and other foreigners in Taiwan. Apart from commemorating Batik Day, the purpose of this event is to introduce batik to foreigners.


At that time I was a volunteer who accompanied the visitors to make batik. I tutored them how to make batik from the start until it finished, then they can take the results home. To be honest..........it was my first time making batik too, hahahahahahaha XD but Alhamdulillah the workshop went well, all visitors, both Indonesians and foreigners, were very happy and excited!
